## Concept

The first installment of the conceptual trilogy. Documents the internal state referred to as **"The Bunker"** — characterized by profound structural dissociation, extreme functional fragmentation, and the rigid enforcement of internal firewalls that keep the trauma holders (EPs) completely segregated from the daily-functioning parts (ANPs).

The "Kohärenz Protokoll" itself is a maladaptive psychological algorithm designed to maintain a desperate facade of normalcy by ruthlessly compartmentalizing emotional data and actively suppressing any internal communication that might trigger a traumatic flashback.

The 16 songs give voice to the distinct, isolated alters **before any integration has occurred**. Each track is a sealed system — the alters do not communicate with each other. This isolation is both the album's aesthetic and its horror.

## Musical Aesthetic

- **Tempo**: Strict, unwavering 100 BPM baseline (the Bunker's enforced order)
- **Primary Genre**: Cold, precise Dystopian Future Synth
- **Secondary**: Ethereal Darkwave (representing the void of dissociation)
- **No**: Warm sounds, organic instruments, emotional resolution
