## Concept

The metaphorical death of Kael's singular host control. The absolute "Shatter Pattern" — core identity fractures into unmanageable multitude. Not literal death but the necessary dissolution of the false singular self. Kael cannot be "one person" anymore; the architecture that maintained that illusion has completely failed. This track is the musical representation of identity decompensation.

Note: This is transformation, not destruction — but it cannot be experienced as anything other than death in the moment.
